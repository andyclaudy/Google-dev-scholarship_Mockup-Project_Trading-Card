Quokka

The quokka has little fear of humans and it is common for quokkas to approach people closely, particularly on Rottnest Island. It is, however, illegal for members of the public to handle the animals in any way, and feeding, particularly of "human food", is especially discouraged as they can easily get sick.

  • Size: 54cm
  • Ability: Climb trees
  • Favorite food: Grass and leaves
  • Origin: Rottnest Island
  • Nickname: Happiest animal

The quokka is a small marsupial similar in appearance to a wallaby or kangaroo, with distinctive short brown coarse hair and lighter underparts.
